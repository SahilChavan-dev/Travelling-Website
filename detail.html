Tour Overview

Experience the magic of Paris with our exclusive 5-day exploration tour. This carefully curated itinerary takes you beyond the typical tourist spots to discover the authentic charm of the City of Lights while still visiting all the iconic landmarks.

Tour Highlights

  • Skip-the-line access to the Eiffel Tower summit
  • Private guided tour of the Louvre Museum
  • Seine River dinner cruise with live music
  • Visit to Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur Basilica
  • Exclusive wine tasting in a Parisian cellar
  • Walking tour of Le Marais district
  • Day trip to Versailles Palace (optional upgrade)

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Welcome to Paris

Arrive at your hotel where you'll meet your tour guide and fellow travelers. After settling in, we'll take an orientation walk through the nearby neighborhood, followed by a welcome dinner at a traditional Parisian bistro.

Day 2: Iconic Landmarks & Louvre

Morning visit to the Eiffel Tower with skip-the-line access to the summit for breathtaking views. After lunch at a local café, enjoy a private guided tour of the Louvre Museum focusing on its masterpieces. Evening at leisure.

Day 3: Montmartre & Seine Cruise

Explore the artistic Montmartre district, visiting Sacré-Cœur Basilica and Place du Tertre. Afternoon free for shopping or optional activities. In the evening, enjoy a gourmet dinner cruise along the Seine River with live music.

Day 4: Le Marais & Wine Tasting

Walking tour through the historic Le Marais district with its stunning architecture and trendy boutiques. Afternoon wine tasting in a traditional Parisian cellar. Optional evening cabaret show (additional cost).

Day 5: Departure or Versailles Extension

Morning at leisure for last-minute shopping or sightseeing. Optional day trip to the Palace of Versailles (additional cost). Transfer to the airport for your departure flight.
